Our Story

We met during our freshman year of high school, instantly becoming best friends by bonding over our love of music. We would often quiz each other by naming albums and expecting the other person to recite the list of songs on the album. These games would often lead down the most ridiculous rabbit holes with us discussing our knowledge of bands or musical artists ranging from the 1950s to the 2000s for hours on end.

Over the years, our interests evolved and we both enrolled in California State University Channel Islands. From there, we helped one another grow into our careers and adulthood. Assisting each other by providing love and support, we both matured in our academics and daily lives.

After graduation, we established stomping grounds in Ventura, Simi Valley, Laguna Beach, and are now settled in Aliso Viejo. During our journey, we tacked on the responsibility of caring for our two pups, Chip and Potato.

We love to spend our time sharing our experiences and interests; whether it is by going to Disneyland, watching the current hit T.V. show, training the dogs, listening to vinyl records, finding niche plant-based recipes to try, or spending time with family and friends.

We have been best friends and more for almost 12 years. Our bond will be forever growing. We are grateful for the people who have shared their experiences with us and helped us get to where we are today.
